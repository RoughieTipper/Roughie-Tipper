🎯 How Scoring Works

Pick the winner of each match. Points are awarded based on the odds — the bigger the upset you tip, the more points you score.

✅
Correct tip on short odds
$1.00–$2.49 = 1pt · standard tipping range
🤑
Roughie Points — tipping an upset
Tip the underdog (odds $2.50+) and they win = extra points. The longer the odds, the more points.
❌
Wrong tip
0 points. Better luck next week 😬
🏠
Missed tip
Forgot to tip? You automatically get the away team.

🎯 How Scoring Works

Pick the winner of each match. Points are awarded based on the odds — the bigger the upset you tip, the more points you score.

✅
Correct tip — favourite
$1.00–$1.99 = 1pt · standard tipping range
🤑
Roughie Points — tipping a big upset
Tip the underdog (odds $2.00+) and they win = extra points on top. The longer the odds, the more points.
❌
Wrong tip
0 points. Better luck next week 😬
🏠
Missed tip
Forgot to tip? You automatically get the away team. So get your tips in!
📅 Odds & Deadlines
📊
Odds are locked in on Wednesday night before each round. They're set once and don't move — even if the live market shifts before kick-off.
⏰
Tips lock at kick-off time. You can change your mind as many times as you like right up until the siren.
📅 Joining Mid-Season
🏉
You can join at any point in the season — no FOMO required. If you've missed rounds, you automatically get the away team for any games you missed.
🤑 Side Bets
😂
Side bets are purely for fun and have zero impact on standings. Challenge a mate to a slab, bragging rights — it's an honour system. 🍺
⚡ Good to Know
🏆
The leaderboard is sorted by total points, then margin accuracy as the tiebreaker.
👀
Tips are hidden until kick-off. No sneaky last-minute copying.
🌐
Global rankings pit every tipper across all comps against each other.
