📅 Odds & Deadlines
📊
Odds are locked in on Wednesday night before each round. They're set once and don't move — even if the live market shifts before kick-off.
⏰
Tips lock at kick-off time. You can change your mind as many times as you like right up until the siren.
📅 Joining Mid-Season
🏉
You can join at any point in the season — no FOMO required. If you've missed rounds, you automatically get the away team for any games you missed.
🤑 Side Bets
😂
Side bets are purely for fun and have zero impact on standings. Challenge a mate to a slab, bragging rights — it's an honour system. 🍺
⚡ Good to Know
🏆
The leaderboard is sorted by total points, then margin accuracy as the tiebreaker.
👀
Tips are hidden until kick-off. No sneaky last-minute copying.
🌐
Global rankings pit every tipper across all comps against each other.
