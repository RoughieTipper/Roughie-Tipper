📖 THE RULES
Everything you need to know — short and sweet
🎯 How Scoring Works

Pick the winner of each match. Simple as that. Points are awarded based on the odds — the bigger the upset you tip, the more points you score.

✅
Correct tip — favourite
$1.00–$1.99 = 1pt · standard tipping range
🤑
Roughie Points — tipping a big upset
Tip the underdog (odds $2.00+) and they win = extra points on top. The longer the odds, the more points. These must be earned — you can earn roughie points on any tip including the automatic away team if you missed that week.
❌
Wrong tip
0 points. Better luck next week 😬
🏠
Missed tip
Forgot to tip? You automatically get the away team. So get your tips in!
📅 Odds & Deadlines
📊
Odds are locked in on Wednesday night before each round. They're set once and don't move — even if the live market shifts before kick-off. What you see Wednesday is what counts.
⏰
Tips lock at kick-off time. You can change your mind as many times as you like right up until the siren — just can't touch them once the game starts.
📅 Joining Mid-Season
🏉
You can join at any point in the season — no FOMO required. If you've missed rounds, you automatically get the away team for any games you missed — same as anyone who forgets to tip.
🤑 Side Bets
😂
Side bets are purely for fun and have zero impact on the competition standings. Challenge a mate to a slab, bragging rights, whatever you like — it's an honour system. The app tracks who accepted what, the rest is between you and your dignity. 🍺
⚡ Good to Know
🏆
The leaderboard is sorted by total points, then correct tips, then margin accuracy as the tiebreaker. Closest margin guess wins the tiebreak.
👀
Tips are hidden until kick-off. No sneaky last-minute copying — once the first game of the round starts, everyone's picks are revealed.
🌐
Global rankings pit every tipper across all comps against each other. Settle the real GOAT debate once and for all.
